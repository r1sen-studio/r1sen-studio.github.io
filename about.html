About Risen 1 Studio

Guiding Passages

Deuteronomy 6:6–9 (NLT)
And you must commit yourselves wholeheartedly to these commands that I am giving you today. Repeat them again and again to your children. Talk about them when you are at home and when you are on the road, when you are going to bed and when you are getting up. Tie them to your hands and wear them on your forehead as reminders. Write them on the doorposts of your house and on your gates.

Joshua 1:8 (NLT)

Vision

A brand aligned with Jesus Christ and led by the Holy Spirit—one that reminds believers of God's presence in everyday life and of His faithfulness, promises, and truths by incorporating Bible verses and biblical truths into useful products, and that helps equip believers by bringing the Good News with them into their everyday lives.

Mission

Rooted in faith, we design and make high-quality, affordable, unique products—stickers, lamps, pins, and more—so believers can hold to God's faithfulness, promises, and truths in everyday lives, express their identity in Jesus Christ, decorate their space, and turn everyday lives into something meaningful and fun by filling the ordinary with reminders of what matters most and reason for joy—and to be a spark of connection for non-believers, inviting them toward what matters most—in the spirit of the Great Commission, equipping believers to make disciples and share the good news wherever they are.

Products

Stickers, lamps, pins, and more—designed and made by us, for your everyday.

We make each piece with care: quality you can touch, designs that stand out, and products that add a little meaning to your space and your stuff. We create these things so you can be reminded that God is creative—and that you are made in His image to create too.

Why Risen 1?

Jesus Christ died for our sins and rose again—He is risen. That truth is our hope and the reason we have joy. We named Risen 1 Studio after it: Risen 1 because Jesus Christ has risen.

That's why we make what we make. We design and produce stickers, lamps, pins, and more so that the reality that He rose again can go with you—into your everyday, your space, your stuff. Our products are meant to remind you of God's faithfulness, His promises, and the good news that Christ has risen, so that what we produce points to Him and helps you live in light of it.

Meet Risen 1 Studio

Risen 1 Studio is stickers, lamps, pins, and more—thoughtfully designed and made to help you express yourself and add meaning to your everyday. We're named for the risen Jesus Christ, rooted in faith, and big on positivity and individuality. Welcome.
